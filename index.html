FitnessJS is a library of exercise physiology equations covering topics such as METs, body composition, VO2Max measurement and 1-RM estimation.

Cardio

Metabolic equivalents (METs) for estimating energy expenditure of over 800 specific activities. Also includes conversion formulas for VO2max and kilocalories (kcals).
Get Started

Composition

Formulas for estimating surface area, fat-free/lean body mass, and indexes for assessing body composition such as BAI, BMI, BSI, corpulence, etc.

Strength

Estimate 1 repetition max (1-RM), relative strength or compare weightlifters across genders and weight classes.

Benefits

  • Authoritative sources All equations included are from reputable sources (cited in documentation)
  • No dependencies No external libraries are required to perform calculations
  • Extendable All classes and methods can be overridden or extended
