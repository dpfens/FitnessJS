All calculations are made using equations in the energy namespace of the FitnessJS library. Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), the minimum amount of energy to keep the systems of the body functioning, is estimated using the Mifflin St. Jeor equation, as it was found by the American Dietetic Association (ADA) to be the most accurate. Resting Energy Expenditure (RMR), is measured using the ‘quick’ formula in the RMR class in the energy namespace. Total Energy Expenditure (TEE) is calculated using the TEEEstimator class. ChildTEE is used for people under 18 years old, and AdultTEE is used for people 18 years of age or older. For information, see the wiki.
